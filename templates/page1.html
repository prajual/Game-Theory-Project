Welcome to the Game

Rules: You will be engaging in 100 rounds of trade with an opponent. In each round, you and your trade partner can either cooperate (allow free trade) or defect (impose a tariff). Your goal is to pick a strategy that earns you as much as possible.

  • If you both cooperate in a given round, you each earn $15.
  • If you both defect, you each earn only $10.
  • If one of you cooperates and the other defects, the defector earns $25 while the cooperator earns only $5.
