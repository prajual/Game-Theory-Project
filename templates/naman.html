Can you win our trade game?

I’ve simplified trade dramatically: You’re engaging in 100 rounds of trade with another player. In each round, you and your trade partner can either cooperate (allow free trade) or defect (impose a tariff). Your goal is to pick a strategy that earns you as much as possible.

If you both cooperate in a given round, you each earn $25 Bn.

If you both defect, you each earn only $15 Bn.

If one of you cooperates and the other defects, the defector earns $60 Bn while the cooperator earns only $5 Bn.
